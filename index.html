Drerick's Mood

Change expression:

This is Drerick. Drerick is automatically set to a neutral mood, however this can be easily changed by clicking the 2 other buttons "Happy and "Fear" where Drericks face comes to life.

By clicking above one Drericks eyebrow you can raise them and when the other is clicked they previous brow returns to reset Drerick face he will need to be Neutral.

Another amazing feature Drerick has is the ability to change his lip colour this can be done by clicking once on his mouth to change to a blue colour.

Drerick's face can be rest by clicking the "Neutral" button

No matter what device you use whether that be table, Phone, laptop or desktop everything works as described, due to this webpage having a responsive layout allowing for multiple resolutions.
